About

Zhaohui Chen
30.07.2023
The team consists of 1 senior engineer at the professor level, 1 professor, 2 associate professors, 1 lecturer, 2 engineers, as well as multiple postdoctoral researchers and graduate students. It is a research group that combines marine observation, equipment development, and mechanism research, with complementary advantages in multiple fields. This enables a close integration of scientific demands and technological research and development. As a core force within the “Ocean Dynamics and Climate” innovation team led by Academician Wu Lixin, our team is dedicated to the implementation of the “Transparent Ocean” observation plan.
