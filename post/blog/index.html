Inhaltsverzeichnis

The team consists of 1 senior engineer at the professor level, 1 professor, 2 associate professors, 1 lecturer, 2 engineers, as well as multiple postdoctoral researchers and graduate students. It is a research group that combines marine observation, equipment development, and mechanism research, with complementary advantages in multiple fields. This enables a close integration of scientific demands and technological research and development. As a core force within the “Ocean Dynamics and Climate” innovation team led by Academician Wu Lixin, our team is dedicated to the implementation of the “Transparent Ocean” observation plan. We emphasize the integration of scientific demands and technological achievements, as well as interdisciplinary exchanges. Actively collaborating with domestic and international institutions, we strive to promote innovative cooperation in marine observation across different organizations and disciplines.
